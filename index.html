God Eater

"Will Humans Become Gods Or Will Gods Become Humans?"

In a world where humans are brought to the brink of extinction by monstrous beings known as "Aragami," we are forced to become something more than human to survive. It is up to you and many of your allies, known as God Eaters, to combat this ever growing threat and bring peace to a world torn apart by fear and despair.
